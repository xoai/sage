## 4. Analysis Framework

**Theme categories to watch for:**
- [Category 1 — connected to research objective 1]
- [Category 2 — connected to research objective 2]
- [Category 3 — emergent themes, open]

**Analysis process:**
1. After each interview: capture key quotes, surprises, and initial
   observations (no interpretation yet)
2. After all interviews: extract individual observations onto separate
   notes
3. Cluster observations into themes (let themes emerge, don't force
   predetermined categories)
4. Name each theme as an insight, not a topic
5. Count: how many participants exhibited each theme?
6. Connect themes to research objectives: did we answer our questions?

**Synthesis template:**

| Theme | # of Participants | Key Quotes | Implication for [Product/Opportunity] |
